Business documents (ERM/COLD)

COLD as a solution is more viable today than it has ever been. The acronym stands for Computer Output to Laser Disk and as the name implies Computer Output is the main ingredient in COLD. As much as the name implies the use of Laser Disks to store the computer output, the fact is that modern COLD systems, like the one QuickFile Pro™ encorporates, do not use the very early technology of Laser Disks. That is why it was recently agreed upon between various COLD vendors and commitees to expand its name to Enterprise Report Management or ERM/COLD, to try to capture the more evolved technology available today.
